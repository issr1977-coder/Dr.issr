About Me

Dr. Illapu Sankara Srinivasa Rao, widely known as Dr. ISSR, is a distinguished educator, researcher, mentor, and one of India’s most respected trainers in Computer Science and Engineering. With expertise across more than 40 subjects, including DBMS, Computer Networks, Operating Systems, Compiler Design, TOC, Distributed Systems, and Cybersecurity, he has shaped the academic and professional journeys of thousands of students across India. Renowned for his dynamic teaching style, real-time examples, shortcuts, and conceptual clarity, Dr. ISSR has trained numerous top performers and single-digit rank holders in GATE examinations. His association with leading national coaching institutes such as Made Easy, Gate Forum, Engineers Circle, TIME, and Unacademy has earned him the reputation of a master trainer and a role model for aspiring engineers. Beyond teaching, he is an active researcher with multiple publications, patents, and technical contributions in Artificial Intelligence, Optimization, IoT, Sensor Networks, and Wireless Communication. He has guided students in hackathons, national innovation challenges, and technical competitions, with several teams winning at prestigious platforms including Smart India Hackathon. Currently pursuing his Post-Doctoral research, Dr. ISSR continues to contribute to the academic community through innovation, mentorship, and leadership. His dedication to student success and passion for teaching has earned him multiple recognitions, including the honor of "Outstanding Performer." With a mission to inspire, educate, and empower, Dr. ISSR remains a symbol of academic excellence and professional integrity.
